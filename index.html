Common Questions
Best government exam after 12th?

NDA (Defence), SSC CHSL (Clerical), RRB Group D (Railways) and state PSC prelims are great options. For professional courses: JEE Main (Engineering) and NEET UG (Medical) are the top picks.

How many UPSC CSE attempts are allowed?

General: 6 attempts (up to age 32). OBC: 9 attempts (up to 35). SC/ST: unlimited (up to 37). EWS: 6 attempts (up to 32).

Can I apply for JEE Main and Advanced same year?

Yes. First qualify JEE Main and rank in the top 2.5 lakh to become eligible for JEE Advanced. Both exams happen in the same academic year (Jan–May).

Is NEET mandatory for all medical colleges?

Yes. Since 2020, NEET UG is the single national entrance for MBBS, BDS, and Ayush admissions across all government and private medical colleges in India.
